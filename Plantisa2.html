Plantisa

UI Design Challenge for Plant E-commerce website


Project

Overview:

Plantisa is a website for online house-plant shopping. Personal challenge to create a purchase userflow for an indoor plant-selling e-commerce website under 2 days.



Goal:

Create a friendly and appealing website where users would feel safe and encouraged to purchase a plant.

Duration:

2 days


Project Type:

UI Design (Web)


Tools Used:

Adobe XD




The Process

Low/Midfi Sketches






Style Tile

Once the Midfi-Protytpes were completed, I created the style tile to keep a consistent theme throughout the website. I used a light blue color to establish user's sense of security, while still giving off a friendly feel.

Lowercase for certain areas:
Creates an approachable atmosphere at first sight.

Dark Overlay over certain images:
Simply for accessibility, as most of my images were on the lighter side.

Hi-fi Prototypes

For my Hifi, I focused on using simple and clean images to match the tone of the website.




Opportunities

Although I was able to ultimately meet my goal, there are certain pages that were not made into this prototype, solely due to that fact that the time duration of this challenge did not allow me to create pages that were lower in priority. This includes account pages, search results page, terms/conditions page, 404 pages, and so on.

Another opportunity I had missed was allowing users to have an option to checkout as prior users (has an account). My current prototype only allows users to checkout as guests, which forces users to enter their Contact, Address, and Payment information again, even if they are a returning user.
