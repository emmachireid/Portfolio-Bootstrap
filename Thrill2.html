Thrill

UI Design Challenge from Goodbrief.io


Project

Challenge Description:

You will be creating a website for a real-estate company named "Thrill". They provide co-working spaces for individuals, and the website's main target is to sell the company's products. under 2 days.



Requisite:

1. Create a Landing Page, Contact Page, Shop Page, Terms of Service Page.
2. Landing page should maximize conversions, and should have "Our Values" section.
3. There should be a CTA to get users to follow the company's social media.
4. Brand Color: Black. Design should be luxurious, yet have a modern feel.


Deadline:

3 days


Project Type:

UI Design (Web)


Tools Used:

Adobe XD




The Process

Low-Fi/Midfi Sketches

As luxurious designs were an untouched area for me, I spent a lot of time sketching out my ideas on paper and XD.
I ended up focusing on the use of bold, raw images throughout the website to give my user an idea of what the place would actually look like, without having to book a tour first (which is what a lot of co-working real estates do, as I learned through my research).
I decided on font styles at this point since I was running out of time and did not have the bandwidth to change this during my Hi-fi.








Style Tile

Once the Midfi-Protytpe was completed, I created the style tile to keep a consistent theme throughout the website. I focused on keeping my colors mostly neutral and dark for the luxurious effect.

Opportunities

While I am satisfied with the general look of the website, there are couple of inconsistencies that I have noticed.

  1. I completely forgot to make an account icon at header for members.
  2. Icons are not all similar and size.
  3. Wasted time working on a page that was not in the requisite (purchase flow)*, due to misunderstanding.
  4. Spacing between certain sections are inconsistent.

*The purchase flow pages mentioned above can be viewed from my Hi-fi Prototype.
