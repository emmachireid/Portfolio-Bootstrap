Thrill

UI Design Challenge from Goodbrief.io


Project

Challenge Description:

You will be creating a website for a real-estate company named "Thrill". They provide co-working spaces for individuals, and the website's main target is to sell the company's services.



Requisite:

1. Create a Landing Page, Contact Page, Shop Page, Terms of Service Page.
2. Landing page should maximize conversions, and should have "Our Values" section.
3. There should be a CTA to get users to follow the company's social media.
4. Brand Color: Black. Design should be luxurious, yet have a modern feel.


Deadline:

3 days


Project Type:

UI Design (Web)


Tools Used:

Adobe XD




The Process

Low-Fi/Midfi Sketches

After going through some research to learn more about how co-working space companies and their services worked, I jumped right into design. To create the luxurious and modern feel, I aimed the design with extra white space, along with a small amount of corner radius to the buttons and the content boxes (along with images).

I skipped on using a jumbotron as header, but instead created search bar that would allow users to explore to choose the type of services that Thrill had to offer.








Style Tile

Once the Midfi Prototype was completed, I created the style tile to keep a consistent theme throughout the website. I focused on keeping my colors mostly neutral and dark for the luxurious effect.

Opportunities

While I am satisfied with the general look of the website, there are couple of inconsistencies that I have noticed.

  1. I completely forgot to make an account icon at header for members.
  2. Icons are not all similar and size.
  3. Wasted time working on a page that was not in the requisite (purchase flow)*, due to misunderstanding.
  4. Spacing between certain sections are inconsistent.

*The purchase flow pages mentioned above can be viewed from my Hi-fi Prototype.
