SNIP

Men's Hair Salon Concept Web design


Project

Overview:

UI Design from Concept; website for a small, modern style men's hair salon.

Duration:

2 days


Project Type:

UI Design (Web)


Tools Used:

Adobe XD




The Process

Research

After visiting a couple of local Men's salon/barber websites, I soon came to realize that most sites fall under a luxurious theme, or a rustic theme.
Additionally, most sites listed their services in heavy text, which I believed to be an opportunity to change.

Goal

I will create a rustic yet modern, minimal and spacious website that is easy to navigate for incoming users.

Midfi Sketches

After deciding the webpages, I went on Adobe XD to create a midfi draft. I focused on bringing large blocks of background color and images to draw in users, while keeping a good amount of white space between for the minimal effect.







Style Tile

Once the Midfi-Protytpe was completed, I created the style tile to keep a consistent theme throughout the website. I focused on keeping the colors neutral yet warm toned, as I still wanted to create a semi-rustic look to the site.

Hi-fi Designs

Final Thoughts

Design Success


01. Creating a minimal design by using bold, large texts. The frequent use of image and minimal text also helped me achieve this theme.

02. The usage of background color blocks allowed me to create sections in the otherwise boring background.

03. The warm tone added friendliness to the designs, steering away from a luxurious design and applied a more rustic and modern theme.

The Takeaway

Out of all of my passion projects, I would conclude that this ended up being one of my favorite designs. The minimal, neutral look with bold text and images makes a statement on its own. With that being said, there are a couple of things I could have worked on:


01. Design mobile first before expanding to web. While there is no research on this, it is highly likely that users look for barbers and salons on their mobile devices.

02. Create more pages, such as an online booking page, blog page, etc.

03. Introduce a single color to add a pop of color to the design to guide the user's eye.

04. The difference between the wording Book Online (for services) and Book Appointment (for consultation) is confusing, and could have been improved.
